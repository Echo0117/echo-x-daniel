# 你我

你的眼睛里，
有满载的星河，
星河落进了苍穹；

你的眼睛里，
有无垠的苍穹，
苍穹映照了冰川；

你的眼睛里，
有广袤的冰川，
冰川融化成海洋；

你的眼睛里
有翻腾的海洋，
海洋从此在我心里再无止息。

你的呼吸里，
有和煦的春风，
春风化做了夏雨；

你的呼吸里，
有绵柔的夏雨，
夏雨打湿了秋叶；

你的呼吸里，
有散落的秋叶，
秋叶呼唤了冬雪；

你的呼吸里，
有飘扬的冬雪，
冬雪从此在我心里漫天纷飞。

你化作我世界里的万物，
又为这个世界添上四季。
从此你我相融合二为一，
我用你的眼睛看流光溢彩；
我用你的呼吸感鸟语花香。

至死不渝。


In your eyes
lies the glimmering star-river,
and the star-river
falls into the firmament.

In your eyes
lies the vasty firmament,
and the firmament
mirrors the glacier.

In your eyes
lies the boundless glacier,
and the glacier
melt into the ocean.

In your eyes
lies the surging ocean,
and ever since, the ocean has never ceased within my heart.

In your breath
flows the gentle spring breeze,
and the spring breeze turns summer rain.

In your breath
flows the tender summer rain,
and the summer rain wets autumn leaves.

In your breath
flows the drifting autumn leaves,
and the autumn leaves howl toward winter snow.

In your breath
flows the dancing winter snow,
and ever since, the winter snow has drifted endlessly across the skies of my heart.

You became the essence of my world,
and given this world its turning seasons.
You and I, no longer two but one,
I see the world’s radiance through your eyes;
I breathe the scent of blossoms and the hush of birds through your breath.

Steadfast, even unto death.
