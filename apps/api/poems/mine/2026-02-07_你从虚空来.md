# 你从虚空来

我有一片虚空，
混沌又明朗，
我不知模样，
却凭灵魂洞悉一切。

我将思想与盼望交织，
全部融入，
长眠等待。

你从虚空里来，
你摘下，
枝头的点翠，
花瓣的晨露，
雨后的芬芳，
溪涧岩石的脆响，

幻化出清明，
从天际划过闪电，
直击灵魂，
消散混沌，
将一切具象化。

你走到我面前，
一眼认出我，
叫出我的名字，
唤醒我的长眠。
道了一声你好。

我的虚空，
从此以后，
阳光普照，
春暖花开。


# You Came from the Primordial Void

I possessed a primordial void,
chaotic, yet lucid.          
I knew no shape,
yet through the soul
I perceived all things.

I wove thought with longing,
folded them back,
and lay in deep and timeless slumber.

You came forth from the primordial void.
You gathered,
the kingfisher blue from budding tips,
the dew of the dawn resting on petals,
the fragrance born after rain, 
the clear ringing of stone against stream.

You shaped them into clarity,
a lightning stroke cleaving the vault of heaven, 
striking the soul,
scattering the chaos,
calling all things into form.

You walked toward me,
recognized me at a glance,
called me by my name,
and woke me from my long sleep.
You said softly,
hello.

From that moment onward,
my primordial void,
was flooded with sunlight,
and the world bloomed into spring.
