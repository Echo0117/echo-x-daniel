# 此年

我若在百年前遇见你，
兵戎战乱，
烽火家书，
歃血为盟。

我若在千年前遇见你，
花灯市集，
吟诗作对，
泛舟湖上。

我若在万年前遇见你，
依山傍水，
男耕女织，
炊烟袅袅。

可非百年，非千年，非万年，
偏偏是此年，
便无战火，无远途，无忧劳。
天时地利，
只为深爱你。


Had I met you a hundred years ago,
in an age of war and strife,
with beacon fires, with letters sent afar,
We shall have sealed our vow in blood.

Had I met you a thousand years ago,
amid lanterns and the evening throng,
We shall have answered verse by verse,
and let our boat drift slow upon the lake.

Had I met you ten thousand years ago,
by mountains leaning into rivers,
I shall weave, and you in the fields
with threads of smoke beckoning you home.

Not a hundred years, nor a thousand, nor ten thousand—
But this year.
No war to divide us, no long road between us, no weary life to endure.
Heaven and earth in perfect accord,
all for this:
that I may deeply love you.
