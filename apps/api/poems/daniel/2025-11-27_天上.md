# 天上

天上出现夜星
我和她相看见
微光冷照指導我入道

天上出现彗星
我和他相看见
光尾如矢軌導我入道

天上出现月亮
我和她相看见
优雅的光指導我入道

天上出现太阳
我和他相看见
有力的光軌導我入道

天下出现晃光
我和她相看见
灿烂的笑招手我入家



To you, my beloved,
I feel your absence in every thought about myself, as every thought about myself has you in my life. Every time I think of the future, I see you there and realize again that you're not with me now (physically). But today is the day we see each other again. Happy is this day. 

Please stay safe and arrive well in my arms later tonight. I will see you at the airport.

Today, I wanted to experiment with poetry and tried some poetry in Chinese. I started with English, but Chinese was smoother and got it to the current stage very quickly. 

I think there are some grammar mistakes and weird word choices, but I hope you get the gist.
I look forward to connecting with you over this poem hopefully. 

Love,
Daniel
