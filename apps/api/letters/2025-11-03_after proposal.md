Dear Mr. and Mrs. Lu, 

I have met your daughter Diyun this June and we have not spent a day without seeing each other since. She has been my best friend,  cherished partner and companion. I value our strong connection emotionally and intellectually, and know her to be the person I want to spend the rest of my life with.  It brings me great joy and happiness to stand before you and ask for your blessings for your daughter's hand in marriage, and your goodwill for our future together. I lament not having had the opportunity to meet you in person before this, but look forward to doing so in the coming year. For now, I reiterate my intentions for the joining of our families:

我想請您給我們一個祝福. 

I look forward to your answer. 

Daniel Gregorievich Itkis.
