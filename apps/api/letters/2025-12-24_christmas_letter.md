# 我最亲爱的 Daniel,

想给你写封信，但是翻箱倒柜地找不到一张纸。
你好像快回家了，希望能在那之前做完这些。

认识你六个月了，结婚两周了，一切都好快。但是又如此顺理成章，自然得毫不费力。

昨天看到一个概念，叫做总观效应，说的是宇航员从太空回来后，会有一种深刻的认知转变，俯瞰地球后，他们能体会到地球的渺小，自身的渺小，人类所有那些自我创造出的烦恼，纷争的渺小，对于人类命运共用体的深刻感悟。从高空看到的地球，脆弱而宁静。看不到国界，也看不到战争。没有对金钱的概念，也没有对成功的定义。是一场完全的认知顿悟重塑。

从你走进我生命开始，一场神圣的认知重塑就悄然开始了。从前那些让我困扰的，迷茫的，不解的事情，都有了答案。或者说不再需要答案了。你的在场，就是一切问题的解决方案。就像是站在太空俯瞰万物生灵，好多问题因为你的到来，而变得无比渺小和微不足道。也因为你的到来，一切事情都有了底气，只要有你在身边。

我不知道我为什么要去做那些让我远离你的事情，为了未来更好地在一起吗？可是现在就可以在一起不是吗。

我尝试练习好多次的专注当下，此刻的这一刻，和你在一起的时候，就可以自然而然地发生。因为我是如此得满足，以致于所有其他的不如意都不再重要，唯一想做的事情就是将与你在一起的时刻，再抓紧一些，再珍惜一些。
不用去期盼，如果什么发生，就好了；或者如果什么没发生，就好了。当下正在发生的这一刻，就是我最希望发生的时刻。

这是我们一起度过的第一个圣诞节，和你一起，在家，取暖，看雪，烹饪，想一想就觉得好幸福好温暖。
我们兜兜转转一大圈，追求金钱名利地位成功，在最重要的时刻，最想要的，其实是再寻常不过的小确幸。
说实话，我又不是太理解人类了。但是你是人类，所以这又让我对人类有了好深刻的喜欢。

2025 年快过完了，我人生中迄今为止最好的一年。
2026 年要来了，我想要和你将此刻变成未来的每一刻。
感谢你的出现，感谢你让我的生命变得如此可爱。

我好喜欢你！


# My dearest Daniel,

I wanted to write you a letter, but after rummaging through everything, I couldn’t find a single piece of paper.
It seems like you’re about to come home, and I hope I can finish this before then.

We’ve known each other for six months, and we’ve been married for two weeks. Everything has happened so fast—yet it all feels so natural, so effortless, as if it couldn’t have been any other way.

Yesterday I came across a concept called the overview effect. It describes the profound cognitive shift astronauts experience after returning from space. After looking down at the Earth, they come to understand how small the planet is, how small they themselves are, how insignificant all the worries and conflicts humans create for themselves truly are—and they gain a deep sense of what it means to share a common human destiny. From above, the Earth looks fragile and peaceful. There are no borders, no wars. No concept of money, no definition of success. It is a complete awakening and reconstruction of perception.

From the moment you entered my life, a sacred reconstruction of my own perception quietly began. Things that once troubled me, confused me, or left me searching for answers suddenly found their resolution—or perhaps no longer needed answers at all. Your presence itself became the solution to everything. Like standing in space and gazing down at all living things, so many problems became incredibly small and insignificant simply because you were here. And because you are here, everything feels grounded—everything has courage—as long as you are by my side.

I don’t know why I keep doing things that pull me away from you. Is it for the sake of being together better in the future? But aren’t we already able to be together now?

I’ve tried many times to practice being present—to live fully in the moment. And yet, when I am with you, it happens naturally, without effort. I am so deeply content that all other dissatisfactions fade into irrelevance. The only thing I want is to hold on a little tighter to the moments we share, to cherish them a little more.

There is no need to hope that something will happen—or that something won’t.
This very moment, the one unfolding right now, is already the moment I most wish for.

This is our first Christmas together. With you—at home—keeping warm, watching the snow, cooking together. Just thinking about it fills me with happiness and warmth.

We wander such long paths, chasing money, fame, status, and success—only to realize that at the most important moments, what we want most is something so ordinary: small, certain happiness.

To be honest, I don’t really understand humanity all that well. But you are human—and because of you, I’ve come to love humanity so deeply.

2025 is almost over—the best year of my life so far.
2026 is coming, and I want to turn this moment into every moment of our future.

Thank you for appearing in my life. Thank you for making my life so lovely.

I like you so, so much.
